## Typography
- **Display/Titles:** Playfair Display (serif), 700-900 weight — elegância clássica
- **Body:** Inter (sans-serif), 400-600 weight — leitura limpa
- **Badges/Labels:** Inter, uppercase, letter-spacing wide
